About Us

Holloware is a C2C (consumer to consumer) platform for our fellow lovers of handmade pottery and ceramics, designed by our team of four: Dow Dodd, Phoebe Thompson, Rebekah Foley-Read and Dominic Nacha. Holloware is a market space for our community of potters and ceramicist to buy and sell their products. The seller will have the ability to post a product with its associated details, buyers can view, leave comments and ask questions about the product/s. Our solution allows sellers to: create products to sell, comment on products, update or edit the products users have posted and delete their own products.
